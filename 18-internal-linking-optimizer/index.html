Turn a pile of pages into a topical-authority graph

Internal linking is how a site builds topical authority and passes equity between pages — not an afterthought "related posts" widget. This maps a sample content set for the fictional Northstar Outfitters site into topic clusters, finds the gaps that quietly cap rankings — orphan pages with no inbound links, pillars their own cluster barely links to, and highly-related pages that were never connected — and recommends specific internal links (from → to) with a reason and a keyword-overlap strength. Similarity is inspectable overlap, not a black-box embedding. Deterministic, client-side; the corpus is a bundled fixture.
