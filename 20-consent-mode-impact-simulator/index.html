How much of your revenue does analytics never see?

Consent declines, Safari/ITP cookie prevention, and ad-blockers each strip conversions before they're ever recorded — and the loss isn't uniform, so it quietly biases budget toward the channels that happen to be trackable. This simulates the observed-vs-actual gap for the fictional Northstar Outfitters store, per channel, using each channel's privacy profile; shows how much Google-style consent-mode modelling claws back; and decomposes the gap by cause. Deterministic expected-value maths — the point is that your numbers are wrong by a knowable amount, so don't optimise to them blind.
