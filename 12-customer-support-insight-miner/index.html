What are your support tickets actually telling you?

Support tickets are not a queue to burn down — they're customer intelligence. This mines the fictional Northstar Outfitters support corpus into operational decisions: theme clusters, sentiment and urgency, product-category friction, content gaps, support-risk customers, automation candidates, and an owner-routed action queue. The expert move is turning recurring tickets into fixes and automations, not counting ticket volume. Deterministic keyword/theme rules, client-side, synthetic identifiers only.
